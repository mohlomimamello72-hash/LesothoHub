Business Tips for Farmers

2. Use Social Media

Promote your products on Facebook, WhatsApp, and Instagram to reach more customers locally and beyond.

3. Maintain Quality

Always provide fresh, clean, and high-quality produce. Quality attracts loyal customers and word-of-mouth promotion.

4. Collaborate with Other Farmers

Team up with fellow farmers to share transport costs, bulk-sell products, and negotiate better deals in markets.

5. Keep Records

Track expenses, income, and inventory. Good record-keeping helps you make informed decisions and grow sustainably.
